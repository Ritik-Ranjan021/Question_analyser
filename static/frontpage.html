Pipeline

How It Works

A local-first RAG pipeline that transforms raw PDFs into actionable study intelligence in seconds.

STEP 01
Ingest Documents

Drop PDF or TXT past-year papers into the data/ folder. PyPDF2 extracts clean text automatically.

PyPDF2 · TXT · PDF
STEP 02
Chunk & Embed

Text is split into overlapping 400–600 token chunks. Each chunk is encoded into a dense semantic vector using all-MiniLM-L6-v2.

sentence-transformers
STEP 03
Build FAISS Index

Normalized vectors are stored in a FAISS inner-product index, enabling millisecond-speed similarity search across thousands of passages.

FAISS · cosine similarity
STEP 04
Retrieve Passages

Your query is embedded and matched against the index. Top-k most relevant passages are returned with full source metadata.

top-k nearest neighbors
STEP 05
Generate Suggestions

Retrieved passages are assembled into a structured prompt. The generator outputs key concepts, practice questions, and resources.

local · HF Inference API
STEP 06
View via CLI or UI

Results are delivered through an interactive web UI or a fast command-line interface — both backed by a clean FastAPI REST endpoint.

FastAPI · static SPA

Surfaces the top 5 most frequently tested concepts from your question corpus automatically.

3 tailored practice questions modeled directly on retrieved past-year content.

Curated Resource Links

Recommended articles, tutorials, and reference pages matched to retrieved topics.

Local-First & Private

Runs entirely on your machine with CPU-only support. No data leaves your device by default.
